## Who I Am

Pat Bennett. 23 years old, turning 24 on August 16, 2026. Based in St. Petersburg, Florida. Full-stack engineer, designer, systems architect, and founder — currently running every layer of a company built from scratch while holding down a full-time day job.

Everything is built with my own hands, my own hours, and my own conviction that the work will pay off if I stay relentless and stay smart. Everything is dedicated to finding the meaning of life and finding freedom for myself.

Man of faith. Prayer and discipline is a foundational part of how I move, decide, and stay grounded. I don't just work — I believe in what I'm building and why.

## The Company — [[Sidechain 2026|SideChain]]

Established in 2025. Automated Growth-as-a-Service (AGaaS) — high-end web development with curated, bespoke designs. The website is the surface; under the hood is the proprietary **SIKWENS** infrastructure: a full revenue generation engine with analytics, content strategy, marketing automation, and AI systems designed to eliminate workflow overload, kill inefficiencies, and generate MRR.

Also: on-site content + media production and strategic consulting — surfacing pain points and guiding clients toward the highest-value tier where everything is automated, integrated, and deployment-ready.

**What makes us dangerous:** 1–2 hour turnaround for full client setup, 45–60 minute turnaround for demo websites. Speed is a weapon — and SIKWENS is the system that wields it.

Partner: Dalton joining for lead-gen and sales. Pat continues to own backend, CI/CD pipeline, frontend framework, design direction, and AI/automation. 80+ skills injected into the SIKWENS agents to keep output precise, detailed, manageable, profitable, low-overhead.

## Philosophy on Success — Core Beliefs

- **Work smarter, then outwork everyone.** Leverage every system + tool + automation, then put in more hours than the person next to you. Intelligence without effort wastes potential. Effort without intelligence wastes time. Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work hard. I do both.
- **Obsessive focus wins.** I don't spread thin. SideChain is the vehicle — everything feeds it. Everything captured in Obsidian for graphing or jotted for recall.
- **Money is secondary. Money is secondary. Money will always be secondary.** Counterintuitive for someone chasing $10M+/year, but: chase the craft → money follows. Chase the money → corners cut, fragile builds.
- **Produce results every single day.** Modest or exceptional — every day moves the needle. Zero-output days are unacceptable.
- **Willpower and sacrifice are the price of entry.** Don't go out on weekends. Don't chase comfort. Sacrificing social life, short-term pleasure, sleep, and 9-5 safety. The cost of staying comfortable is higher than the cost of grinding.
- **Pray about it.** Before strategy, before hustle, before code — pray. Faith is foundation, not a separate track.
- **Read something new every day.** Knowledge compounds. Daily reading for a decade → different level than the person who stopped after school.
- **Invest while you build.** ETFs, low-risk/high-reward. Building income + building wealth.
- **Keep everything manageable.** Ambition without structure → disaster. Push hard, build systems that keep complexity from crushing me.

## The Bottom Line

I'm 23, building a company that changes how businesses operate, holding a full-time job while I do it, refusing to let comfort or convenience slow me down. SideChain is real. SIKWENS works. The only thing between where I am and where I'm going is **time, discipline, sacrifice, and execution**.

No permission needed. No perfect conditions needed. Keep showing up. Keep building. Keep learning. Keep health in check.

The goal isn't money. The goal is **freedom** — for me, for my mother, for my closest peers, and for the businesses that trust me with their growth.

Everything else is noise.
